How It Works

1

Click the menu bar icon

DevCleaner lives in your Mac menu bar — always accessible, never in your way. A single click triggers a scan of all known developer and AI cache locations.

2

Review what can be safely removed

The scan lists every cache folder with its size, sorted by impact. Each item is tagged “safe to delete” or “delete with caution” based on built-in heuristics — no guesswork.

3

One click to reclaim gigabytes

Hit “Clean All Safe Items” and watch disk space come back. DevCleaner only removes files marked safe — your projects, settings, and essential data stay untouched.
