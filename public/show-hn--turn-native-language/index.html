How It Works

1

Paste any YouTube URL

Copy the link of any video in your target language — a vlog, interview, podcast, or tutorial. LingoPaste grabs the audio and optional subtitles automatically using yt-dlp.

2

AI extracts 10 power sentences

Whisper transcribes the audio (if no captions), then GPT-4o-mini selects the 10 most useful, high-frequency, or tricky sentences. For each one we generate: original text, translation, pronunciation guide, and context note.

3

Download your learning pack

You get a printable PDF flashcard deck (ready for Anki or physical cards) plus an MP3 shadowing file with original audio + pauses + beeps. Study offline, anywhere, without staring at a screen.
