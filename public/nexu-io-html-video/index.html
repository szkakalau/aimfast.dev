Why Programmatic Video Matters Right Now

The html-video project exploded on GitHub — 3,403 stars in 25 days, growing at 136 stars per day. Developers are desperate for a way to generate video programmatically without paying per-frame fees. Existing solutions like Remotion Cloud charge $0.01 per frame — a 30-second video at 30fps costs $9. That's insane for AI agents that need to generate hundreds of videos.

Current alternatives are either too complex (self-hosting a full rendering pipeline) or too expensive (cloud rendering services with unpredictable bills). The html-video project proved that HTML-to-video is technically feasible and fast — but it's still a CLI tool. Developers need a simple API they can call from their agent, their CI/CD pipeline, or their product. That's exactly what we built.

This is the perfect timing: the open-source community has validated the approach, the pain point is fresh, and the demand for agent-friendly APIs is at an all-time high. By offering a flat monthly price with no per-render fees, we remove the two biggest barriers: complexity and cost.

How It Works

1

Choose a template

Pick from 21 ready-to-use templates — product demos, social media clips, intros, and more. Each template accepts dynamic data fields so you can customize text, colors, and assets without touching HTML.

2

Send your data via API

Make a single HTTP POST to /render with your template name and a JSON payload. Your AI agent, backend, or CI pipeline can call it directly. No SDK required — just a REST client.

3

Get your MP4 back

We render the video asynchronously and notify you via webhook or you can poll the status endpoint. Download the final MP4 and use it anywhere — social media, product demos, email campaigns.
