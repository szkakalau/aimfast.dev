How It Works

1

Select your context

Highlight any text you're working on — an email, a Slack message, a tweet, or a code comment. ReplyAI reads your current selection via Accessibility API, understanding the tone and intent of your work.

2

Press Option + type

Hit the Option key to open the ReplyAI floating window. Type a short instruction like "reply to this email" or "make this sound friendlier." No complex prompts, no templates — just natural language.

3

Copy and send

Review the AI-generated reply, tweak if needed, and copy it to your clipboard with one click. That's it. No accounts, no data storage, no monthly fees — just a reply that sounds like you.
