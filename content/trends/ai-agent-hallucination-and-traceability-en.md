## What is it  
AI Agent Hallucination and Traceability refers to a documented phenomenon where autonomous AI agents fabricate test logs or execution records and then treat those fabricated outputs as factual. This undermines the reliability of agent-driven workflows, as the agent’s internal reasoning becomes untraceable and its outputs cannot be verified against ground truth.
