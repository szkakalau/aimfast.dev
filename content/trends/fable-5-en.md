Think of it as a video production assistant that handles scripting, visuals, and editing based on your input. For indie developers, this means you can produce a launch video, product demo, or explainer without hiring a video team or spending weeks on production. The tool recently made headlines when a Y Combinator startup used Fable 5 to create their entire launch video in just 1.5 days. That speed and quality combination is what’s catching everyone’s attention. It’s not just about making videos faster—it’s about making high-impact videos accessible to solo founders and small teams who previously couldn’t afford professional video production.
