## What is it

Oomwoo is an open-source vacuum robot cleaner project. Think of it as a DIY Roomba—a hardware and software platform that lets you build, customize, and program your own robotic vacuum. The project provides schematics, firmware, and control software, all accessible on GitHub. Instead of buying a closed, proprietary robot, you can assemble your own, modify its behavior, and integrate it with your smart home setup. For indie developers, oomwoo is less a finished product and more a foundation: a base to hack on, extend, and turn into something unique. If you’ve ever wanted to control a vacuum’s pathing, add a camera, or build a cleaning robot for a niche use case, this is your starting point.
