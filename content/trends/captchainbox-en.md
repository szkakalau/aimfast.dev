## What is it

Captchainbox is an email tool that flips the usual inbox dynamic on its head. Instead of letting anyone send you a message freely, it forces the sender to complete a small task or challenge before their email lands in your inbox. Think of it as a CAPTCHA for your email—senders prove they're human and genuinely interested in reaching you. For indie developers, this means cutting down on spam, cold pitches, and low-effort outreach without losing important messages. It's a lightweight layer of friction that prioritizes quality over quantity in your inbox.

## Why now

Email overload is at an all-time high, especially for founders and developers who share their addresses publicly. Spam filters have gotten better, but they still let through a flood of automated cold emails and marketing blasts. At the same time, users are becoming more protective of their attention and time. The rise of "pay to email" services and proof-of-work systems shows a clear demand for tools that make senders invest effort. Captchainbox taps into this shift by offering a simple, non-monetary barrier. It's emerging now because the tech is trivial to implement, and indie devs are hungry for practical ways to reclaim focus.
