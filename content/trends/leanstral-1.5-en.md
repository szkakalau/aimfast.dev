## What is it

Leanstral 1.5 is an open-source AI model released by Mistral under the permissive Apache 2.0 license. Think of it as a leaner, more accessible alternative to large proprietary models like GPT-4. For indie developers, this means you can download, fine-tune, and deploy a capable language model on your own infrastructure without paying per-token fees or worrying about vendor lock-in. It’s designed for efficiency, running well on consumer-grade hardware while delivering solid performance on coding, reasoning, and text generation tasks. The model continues Mistral’s strategy of releasing powerful open-weight models that compete with closed-source alternatives, making advanced AI more accessible to small teams and solo founders.
