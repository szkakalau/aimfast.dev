## What is it

Kimi K3 is an open-source AI model released by Moonshot AI, boasting an enormous 2.8 trillion parameters. In plain terms, it’s a massive language model that matches or even beats Anthropic’s closed-source Fable 5 on key benchmarks like SWE-bench. For indie developers, this means access to frontier-level AI performance without vendor lock-in or per-token fees. You can download, fine-tune, and deploy it yourself, potentially slashing costs compared to proprietary APIs. The model’s open-source nature also allows full customization for niche use cases, making it a powerful tool for building AI-driven products on your own terms.
