## What is it

Markwise is a lightweight Chrome extension that lets you annotate, highlight, and take notes directly on web pages. It also remembers your annotations after a page refresh, so you never lose your work. Think of it as a personal markup layer for the entire web. For indie developers, it’s a simple productivity tool that solves a real pain point: keeping track of information while browsing.

## Why now

Browser-based productivity tools are surging as remote work and online research become the norm. Users are tired of switching between apps to save snippets or highlights. Markwise taps into the need for lightweight, in-browser solutions that don’t require a full note-taking ecosystem. The rise of AI-powered summarization and local-first data storage also makes this kind of tool more viable and appealing.
