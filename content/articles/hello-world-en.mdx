## What is KAKAOPC Intel?

KAKAOPC Intel scans **14 signal sources** every day — Hacker News, GitHub Trending, Reddit, Product Hunt, V2EX, Xiaohongshu, and more — to find validated product opportunities for indie developers.

## How It Works

1. **Collect** — 14 sources scanned daily for emerging signals
2. **Score** — E-P-A framework evaluates evidence, plain-English translation, and actionability
3. **Report** — One validated opportunity every morning with a build plan

## The E-P-A Framework

Every signal goes through three filters:

- **Evidence anchoring** — Numbers over adjectives. "417 HN comments + 38 Lobsters" not "people are discussing X"
- **Plain-English translation** — What does this signal mean for a builder? Who pays? What do you build?
- **Counter-view** — What would make this judgment wrong? If you can't answer that, you don't understand it yet.

## Who Is This For?

Chinese indie developers and bootstrappers who have technical skills but lack a systematic way to discover and validate product ideas. If you've ever asked "what should I build?" — this is for you.
