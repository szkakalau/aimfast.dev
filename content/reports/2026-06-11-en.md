### Key Open-Source AI Progress

#### OpenSpec — Spec-Driven Development

🔍 **Signal**: Fission-AI/OpenSpec hit 54,072 stars on GitHub, making it the hottest open-source AI project today.

**Plain English**: OpenSpec's philosophy: instead of telling AI in natural language "write a user login feature," write a spec file describing "User login feature needs: 1. Email/password login 2. Third-party OAuth 3. Password reset." AI generates code from the spec, not from a prompt.

**Key Judgment**: This is a key step in AI coding moving from "chaos" to "engineering." If you're building AI coding tools, you must study OpenSpec.

**Counter-View**: OpenSpec is currently a TypeScript project with limited support for other languages.

**Counter-Intuitive Point**: The AI market isn't cooling; it's moving from the "excitement phase" to the "pickiness phase." Picky consumers are the best consumers — they're willing to pay for quality.
