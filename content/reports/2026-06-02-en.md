**MVP stays manual**: Google Form + Markdown report is enough. Don't jump straight to a full auto-platform.

### Today's Most Counter-Intuitive Finding

> **The hotter AI coding tools get, the bigger the demand for AI API cost management tools.**

Intuitively, gstack/superpowers blowing up should push you to build "better AI coding frameworks." But counter-intuitively — **while everyone's teaching AI to write more code, nobody's solving the "write too much, bill explodes" problem.**

The logic behind this counter-intuition:
1. gstack and superpowers users are **heavy users** — their AI coding usage is 10x that of average users
2. Heavy users' bills are 10x that of average users
3. They feel the pain more than anyone
4. But there's no cost management tool for indie developers on the market

That's the Builder's opportunity.
