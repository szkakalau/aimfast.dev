- **Single Audit Report**: $19/report (for one repo or core module)
- **Monthly Monitoring**: $29/month (weekly auto-scan with incremental reports)
- **First Version**: Use Google Form to collect repo links, manually run Shannon, generate a report in Markdown, and export as PDF. **Don't write code.**

**Fastest Validation Path**:

Therefore, your value isn't in "using Shannon" but in "manual verification + readable report." You must invest time verifying results for the first 10 clients to build trust.
